    Thoughts on Web Acessability

    Web accessabily is important for many reasons, one being so if you are selling a product, potential customers will be able to locate and find your website. If your website is hard to find, no one is going to waste their time trying to find it.

    Another reason is because it promotes usability. If the website is hard to navigate, people will not want to visit or use your website.
