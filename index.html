    Thoughts on Web Acessability

    Web accessabily is important for many reasons, one being so if you are selling a product, potential customers will be able to locate and find your website. If your website is hard to find, no one is going to waste their time trying to find it.

    Another reason is because it promotes usability. If the website is hard to navigate, people will not want to visit or use your website.

    Progressive Enhancment

    Progressive enhancment is the art of making content readily available to the masses despite what device they may be using. Progressive enhancement helps designers with conveying a predictable and solid experience across various stages, and programs. Progressive enhancement works in 3 stages. A solid foundation, the content, and the design. Which boils down to html, css, and javascript.
